How to get started

Unlike many outdoor sports, getting started with bouldering is surprisingly simple. All you need are climbing shoes, chalk, and a positive attitude—most gyms even rent equipment for beginners. No prior experience or complex gear required!

Know Before You Go

Before you start climbing, learn proper falling techniques and always check your landing zone. Taking basic safety precautions and learning gym etiquette will help ensure a fun and injury-free experience.

The simple steps to getting started

  1. Find a bouldering gym near you.
  2. Rent or invest in a pair of climbing shoes.
  3. Learn basic bouldering techniques.
  4. Start with easy routes and gradually increase difficulty.
  5. Join a bouldering community or club.
